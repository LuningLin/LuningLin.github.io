I am a Ph.D. candidate in Electronic Science and Technology at [Zhejiang University](https://www.zju.edu.cn/english/) (expected 2026), advised by [Prof. Zhiguo Shi](https://person.zju.edu.cn/shizg) (IEEE Fellow). I am a member of the [NESC Group](http://nesc.zju.edu.cn/) at the State Key Laboratory of Industrial Control Technology. From September 2024 to September 2025, I was a visiting Ph.D. student at the [University of Pisa](https://www.unipi.it/index.php/english), Italy, co-supervised by [Prof. Fulvio Gini](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=eKbNewIAAAAJ) (IEEE Fellow) and [Prof. Maria Sabrina Greco](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=bZXCsFMAAAAJ) (IEEE Fellow).

My research focuses on integrated sensing and communication (ISAC), array signal processing, and wireless communication. My research findings have been featured in journals such as IEEE TSP, IEEE TAES, and IEEE SPL, and presented at flagship conferences including ICASSP and RadarConf. I am committed to academic service as an independent reviewer for journals including IEEE TSP, TMTT, and TVT.
